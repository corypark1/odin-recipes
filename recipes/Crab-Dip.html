Crab Dip

Description

This crab dip recipe is perfect for parties! It's easy to make and delicious served hot in a warm bread bowl.

Ingredients

  • 2 (6 ounce) cans crabmeat, drained and flaked
  • 11 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 5 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • â…› teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (1 pound) loaf round, crusty Italian bread

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Mix: Combine crabmeat, cream cheese, mayonnaise, onion, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl; spread mixture into a 1-quart baking dish.
    3. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, cut a circle in the top of the bread; scoop out the inside to create a bread bowl. Tear the removed bread into pieces for dipping.
    4. Stir: Stir baked crab dip and spoon into bread bowl. Place bread bowl and chunks of bread on a medium baking sheet; bake for an additional 10 minutes. Serve hot.
    5. Enjoy!
