American Lasagna

Description

This classic American Lasagna is not only appealing to the eye but a delight to your taste buds. This meaty lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, and cheese. The meat sauce is made with ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 2 ½ teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 12 dry lasagna noodles
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
